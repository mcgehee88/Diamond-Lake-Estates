Why Pay Your Dues?

Your annual dues support the heart of our community. Every dollar helps us organize neighborhood events, maintain our beautiful lake and common areas, and give residents a voice on issues that matter.

What Your Dues Fund:

  • Community Events – Annual gatherings, social activities, and neighborhood celebrations
  • Lake & Grounds Maintenance – Keeping Diamond Lake pristine for everyone to enjoy
  • Neighborhood Improvements – Road work, landscaping, and infrastructure upgrades
  • Communications – Monthly newsletters and emergency alerts to keep you informed
  • Community Voice – Our civic league represents residents' interests with city government

What Paying Members Get

  • Access to the lake and playground
  • Swimming and fishing privileges
  • Ability to invite guests to use the lake
  • Household membership – covers your family
  • Voting rights on neighborhood decisions
  • Eligibility to serve on the Board or committees
  • A voice in how neighborhood property is maintained and improved

Your dues help maintain our shared spaces and protect neighborhood assets for everyone.

Other Payment Options

You can pay with cash or check. For in-person payments, please coordinate with us at the next meeting or via email at contact@diamondlakeestates.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can be a member?

Any resident of Diamond Lake Estates can join. Membership is based on residency, so you don't need to be a homeowner.

Is my payment tax-deductible?

Yes. Diamond Lake Estates Civic League is a 501(c)(7) nonprofit organization. Consult your tax professional about deductibility.

How many membership cards can I get?

You can request up to 5 membership cards per household. After payment, email contact@diamondlakeestates.com to specify how many you need.

Still have questions?

Contact our treasurer: contact@diamondlakeestates.com
